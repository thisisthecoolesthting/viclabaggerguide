# Harley Fender Tip Lights: A Classic Lighting Upgrade  

## Why Upgrade to Fender Tip Lights?  

Fender tip lights are a subtle yet impactful lighting upgrade for Harley-Davidson bagger and touring motorcycles. These small but bright LED lights mount on the rear fender tips, enhancing visibility, improving safety, and adding a touch of classic style. Unlike bulky auxiliary lights, fender tip lights blend seamlessly with the bike’s lines while providing functional benefits:  

- **Increased Visibility** – Makes your bike more noticeable to drivers behind you, especially in low-light conditions.  
- **Custom Aesthetic** – Available in various finishes (chrome, black, color-matched) to complement your bike’s look.  
- **Plug-and-Play Installation** – Many kits integrate with existing wiring harnesses, minimizing modifications.  

Whether you ride a Road Glide, Street Glide, or Electra Glide, fender tip lights are a practical and stylish addition.  

## Types of Fender Tip Lights  

### **LED vs. Incandescent**  
Most modern fender tip lights use **LED technology** for brighter output, lower power draw, and longer lifespan compared to incandescent bulbs. LEDs also offer crisp, instant illumination—critical for brake and running light functions.  

### **Single-Function vs. Multi-Function**  
- **Single-Function** – Typically serve as running lights only.  
- **Multi-Function** – Combine running, brake, and turn signal capabilities (some even feature sequential turn signals for a premium look).  

### **OEM-Style vs. Aftermarket**  
- **OEM-Style** – Designed to match factory Harley lighting for a seamless appearance.  
- **Aftermarket** – May offer unique designs, such as smoked lenses or integrated reflectors.  

## Key Features to Consider  

When shopping for fender tip lights, focus on these critical aspects:  

1. **Brightness & Visibility** – Look for high-lumen LEDs with a wide viewing angle.  
2. **Weather Resistance** – Ensure the housing is sealed (IP67 or higher) to withstand rain and road debris.  
3. **Compatibility** – Verify fitment for your specific Harley model (some kits are designed for baggers only).  
4. **Wiring & Connectors** – Plug-and-play harnesses simplify installation; avoid units requiring splicing.  
5. **DOT/SAE Compliance** – Legally compliant lights prevent issues during inspections.  

## Installation Tips  

Most fender tip light kits are designed for straightforward installation, but these tips ensure a smooth process:  

- **Check Existing Wiring** – Some touring models have pre-wired fender tip connectors under the seat or fender.  
- **Use Threadlocker** – Prevents vibration-induced loosening of mounting hardware.  
- **Test Before Final Mounting** – Verify light function before securing all bolts.  
- **Seal Connections** – Apply dielectric grease to connectors to prevent corrosion.  

For bikes without pre-wired provisions, a relay or additional wiring may be needed—consult a service manual if unsure.  

## What to Look for When Buying  

Before purchasing, ask these questions:  

- **Does it match my bike’s lighting system?** (e.g., CANBUS-friendly for newer models)  
- **Is the finish durable?** (powder-coated or marine-grade finishes resist rust)  
- **Does it include all necessary hardware?** (some kits omit brackets or bolts)  
- **Are there user reviews confirming reliability?** (look for feedback on long-term performance)  

Opt for reputable brands known for Harley-specific lighting upgrades, and avoid cheap, generic units that may fail prematurely.  

## Final Thoughts  

Fender tip lights are a smart upgrade for Harley bagger and touring riders who value safety and style. With plug-and-play options available, installation is often a simple weekend project. Whether you prioritize increased visibility or a cleaner rear-end aesthetic, the right set of fender tip lights can elevate both function and form.  

For riders who frequently tour or commute at night, pairing these with additional lighting (like LED auxiliary brake lights) further enhances safety. No matter your choice, this small upgrade delivers big benefits.
