## Custom Dynamics vs. Hogworkz: Harley Lighting Showdown  

When upgrading your Harley-Davidson bagger or touring bike’s lighting, two brands consistently stand out: **Custom Dynamics** and **Hogworkz**. Both specialize in high-performance LED lighting solutions, from turn signals and brake lights to accent lighting and full lighting kits. But which one is right for your ride? Let’s break down their strengths, product offerings, and key differences to help you decide.  

### **Brand Overview**  

**Custom Dynamics**  
Known for innovation and reliability, Custom Dynamics focuses on **plug-and-play LED lighting** with advanced features like sequential turn signals, built-in load resistors, and ultra-bright output. Their products are designed for seamless integration with Harley’s CAN bus system, reducing the risk of error codes.  

**Hogworkz**  
Hogworkz emphasizes **affordability and aggressive styling**, offering a mix of classic and modern designs. Their lighting kits often include smoked or clear lenses for a custom look, and they provide extensive options for baggers, touring models, and street bikes.  

### **Product Comparison**  

#### **1. LED Turn Signals & Brake Lights**  
- **Custom Dynamics**  
  - **ProBeam™ LED Modules**: Ultra-bright, with options for sequential or standard flashing.  
  - **Plug-and-Play**: No cutting or splicing required on most models.  
  - **Built-in Load Equalizers**: Prevents hyper-flashing without extra resistors.  

- **Hogworkz**  
  - **Smoked/Clear Lenses**: Popular for a stealthy or custom appearance.  
  - **Direct Fit Replacements**: Some models require minor wiring adjustments.  
  - **Budget-Friendly**: Often priced lower than premium competitors.  

**Best For:**  
- **Custom Dynamics** if you want **plug-and-play reliability** and advanced features.  
- **Hogworkz** if you prefer **custom styling** on a budget.  

#### **2. Accent & Underglow Lighting**  
- **Custom Dynamics**  
  - **Arc™ LED Strips**: Flexible, waterproof, and controllable via Bluetooth or handlebar switches.  
  - **Color-Shifting Options**: Some kits offer RGBW for full customization.  

- **Hogworkz**  
  - **Basic LED Kits**: Single-color options (red, blue, white) with simple on/off or dimming controls.  
  - **Easy Installation**: Often uses adhesive backing for quick mounting.  

**Best For:**  
- **Custom Dynamics** if you want **smart controls and color versatility**.  
- **Hogworkz** if you need **simple, budget-friendly accent lighting**.  

#### **3. Headlights & Auxiliary Lights**  
- **Custom Dynamics**  
  - **Triple Play™ Headlights**: Combines high/low beam and DRL in one unit.  
  - **High-Output Projectors**: Designed for maximum visibility without blinding oncoming traffic.  

- **Hogworkz**  
  - **LED Projector Headlights**: Smoked or chrome housings for a custom look.  
  - **Fog/Driving Light Kits**: Bolt-on auxiliary lighting for improved nighttime visibility.  

**Best For:**  
- **Custom Dynamics** if you prioritize **performance and all-in-one functionality**.  
- **Hogworkz** if you want **stylish housings and auxiliary lighting**.  

### **Installation & Compatibility**  
- **Custom Dynamics**  
  - Most kits are **true plug-and-play**, especially for newer Harley models with CAN bus systems.  
  - Some advanced features (like Bluetooth control) may require additional modules.  

- **Hogworkz**  
  - Some models need **minor wiring modifications** (e.g., adding resistors for turn signals).  
  - Generally compatible with most Harley baggers and touring bikes, but always check fitment guides.  

### **Durability & Warranty**  
- **Custom Dynamics**  
  - Known for **military-grade components** and extensive weatherproofing.  
  - Typically offers **longer warranties** (some products up to 5 years).  

- **Hogworkz**  
  - Durable but may not have the same level of weather sealing.  
  - Warranties are usually **shorter (1-2 years)** but adequate for most riders.  

### **What to Look for When Buying Harley Lighting**  
1. **Compatibility** – Ensure the kit matches your bike’s year, model, and electrical system (especially CAN bus).  
2. **Brightness & Visibility** – Look for high-lumen output and proper beam patterns for safety.  
3. **Ease of Installation** – Plug-and-play is ideal; avoid kits requiring complex wiring if you’re not experienced.  
4. **Build Quality** – Check for waterproof ratings (IP67 or higher) and heat-resistant materials.  
5. **Legal Compliance** – Some states restrict certain colors (e.g., red/blue underglow); stick to DOT-approved lighting where required.  

### **Final Verdict**  
- **Choose Custom Dynamics** if you want **premium performance, smart features, and hassle-free installation**.  
- **Choose Hogworkz** if you prefer **affordable, stylish lighting with a custom look**.  

Both brands deliver solid upgrades, so your decision ultimately comes down to **budget, desired features, and installation preferences**. Whichever you pick, upgrading your Harley’s lighting will enhance visibility, safety, and curb appeal.
